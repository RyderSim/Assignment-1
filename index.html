Biography
Hunter "Simmer" Sim, is widely regarded as one of the most annoying players to play against within the HEO. Born in Contance Bay, Ottawa, Ontario, Hunter displayed a alarmingly high work ethic from a young age, playing 2 years up with his older brother Ryder Sim. Despite his smaller size he dominates every player physcially, creating a matrix of annoyance as the young left winger slashes and finishes his checks.
